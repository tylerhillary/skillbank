Community Guidelines

Welcome to Skill Bank! To ensure a safe, respectful, and productive environment for everyone, we ask all members to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Be Respectful: Treat all members with courtesy and respect. Harassment, bullying, and hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • Be Honest: Represent your skills and experience accurately. Do not mislead others about your abilities or what you can teach.
  • Stay On Topic: Keep discussions and sessions focused on learning and teaching skills.
  • No Soliciting: Do not use the platform for commercial solicitation or to promote external businesses unless it is directly related to a skill you are teaching.
  • Protect Privacy: Do not share personal or confidential information about yourself or others without consent.
  • Report Violations: If you see something that violates our guidelines, please report it to our support team immediately.

By following these guidelines, you help us create a positive and trustworthy community for everyone. Thank you for being a part of Skill Bank!
